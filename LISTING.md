## Tagline (80 chars max)
Generate branded cover pages across multiple Figma pages in one click.

## Description

**Cover Generator** creates polished 1920×1080 cover frames for your Figma files — perfect for keeping your file library organized and professional.

### What it does

- Generates a branded cover with your project title, team name, badge, contributors, and date
- Supports **multi-page generation** — apply covers to multiple pages at once using the built-in page checklist
- Automatically sets the cover as your **file thumbnail** so it looks great in your Figma library
- Saves a named version to your file history

### Customization

- **6 gradient presets**: Blue, Purple, Green, Dark, Sunset, Ocean
- **Custom gradients**: Pick any two colors
- **Badge labels**: Design, Research, Prototype, Engineering, or your own custom label
- **Contributors bar**: Optional bottom bar crediting your design POCs

### How to use

1. Run the plugin from the Plugins menu
2. Choose a gradient, enter your team name and badge
3. Select which pages should get covers
4. Click **Generate Cover** — done!
